A Course To Teach Anyone About Offshore Wind

Within this course are seven modules, each covering a different concept about offshore wind. These concepts build on one another, and come together to give on a complete understanding about offshore wind's purpose, advantages, and execution.

Course Plan

This course begins with an overall introudction to offshore wind and goes on to describe each component of an offshore wind farm and goes into detail on why they are essential to the transition of 100% renewable energy. The potential of energy production from high speed winds out at sea will provide suffiecient power to ___ of the population.
